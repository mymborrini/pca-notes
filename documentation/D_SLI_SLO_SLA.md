# SLI SLO and SLA

Spiegazione generale
INCORRECT: “An agreement made between two or more parties about aspects of a service such as its availability” is incorrect as this is the definition of a Service Level Agreement (SLA).

CORRECT: “A goal regarding an aspect of a service such as its availability” is the correct answer.

INCORRECT: “An availability metric” is incorrect. Availability metrics are often used to define Service Level Indicators (SLIs) which in turn are used in the creation of Service Level Objective (SLOs).

INCORRECT: “A latency metric” is incorrect. Latency metrics are often used in the creation of Service Level Indicators (SLIs) which in turn are used in the creation of Service Level Objectives (SLOs).
